About Gemini Pediatrics

Welcome to Gemini Pediatrics, a trusted pediatric clinic serving the families of the Raleigh-Durham-Chapel Hill area. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, compassionate, and personalized healthcare for children from birth through adolescence. We believe in building strong, long-lasting relationships with our patients and their families, ensuring every child receives the best possible start in life.

Our clinic is conveniently located near the heart of the Research Triangle Park, offering a welcoming and child-friendly environment. We are committed to staying at the forefront of pediatric medicine, utilizing the latest knowledge and technology to deliver exceptional care. At Gemini Pediatrics, your child's health and well-being are our top priority.

Our Services

We offer a full range of pediatric services to meet the growing needs of your child, including:

  • Well-Child Visits & Annual Physicals
  • Immunizations & Vaccinations
  • Newborn & Infant Care
  • Diagnosis & Treatment of Acute Illnesses
  • Chronic Disease Management (e.g., asthma, allergies)
  • School, Camp, and Sports Physicals
  • Developmental & Behavioral Screenings
  • Adolescent Health Services
  • Nutritional Counseling
  • Mental Health & Wellness Support

Meet Our Doctors

Dr. Anya Sharma, MD, FAAP

Dr. Sharma is a board-certified pediatrician with over 15 years of experience dedicated to serving the families of North Carolina. She earned her medical degree from Duke University School of Medicine and completed her residency at UNC Children's Hospital. Her passion lies in preventive care and early childhood development, with a special interest in managing chronic conditions like asthma and allergies. Known for her gentle demeanor and clear communication, Dr. Sharma strives to empower parents with the knowledge they need to make the best health decisions for their children. She is an active member of the American Academy of Pediatrics and lives in Cary with her family.

Dr. Ben Carter, MD, MPH

A native of Raleigh, Dr. Carter is thrilled to be practicing in his hometown. He received his medical degree from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and completed his pediatric residency at Boston Children's Hospital. He also holds a Master of Public Health, which informs his holistic approach to patient care, focusing on the social and environmental factors that impact a child's health. Dr. Carter is particularly passionate about adolescent medicine and sports-related injuries, helping young athletes stay healthy and active. He enjoys volunteering at local schools to promote healthy habits and is a die-hard fan of the Carolina Hurricanes.
